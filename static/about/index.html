About

squidscroll is a minimalist, media-focused experience for reddit.

It's designed to look best on larger screens like a tablet or monitor but it'll work great on mobile too. Check out r/EarthPorn for a good example!

You can login with reddit to see your home page or custom feeds.

The app is currently in beta. More features are coming soon! Follow @sharkdad on Twitter for updates.
